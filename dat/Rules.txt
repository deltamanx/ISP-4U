Use the mouse to aim the ball.
The further away your mouse is from the ball the faster it will launch.
Blue Diamagnets repel, pushing you away from them.
Red Paramagnets attract, pulling you towards them.
Wood Blocks don't attract or repel.
You have to hit the target with the ball to win.
If you hit a magnet or block, you'll bounce off.
You get points every time you bounce off an object.
The faster you complete a level, the higher your score.
If your ball stops, you lose and must restart.
You can restart a level by pressing 'r'.
You can quit to the main menu by pressing 'q'.
You can pause and unpause the game by pressing 'p'.
